Undertale: Judgemental Justice

Background

You are the seventh human to fall into the underground. You started slaying the monsters in your path up until you reached the last corridor. Seeing as you done this route many times, you thought you were well aware about what happens next. Sans and his common speech until he dies. But then, once to walk to where sans was, you find someone next to them. You don't recognize them, but they sure recognize you, down to your very name. Will you be able to prevail and slay both Sans and the other being? Or will you die trying, forced to RESET and try again? Time will tell.
